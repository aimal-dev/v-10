Foundation in Built Environment

This programme is made for the future architect, city developer, landscape designer, civil engineer or urban designer. If you have a creative mind with ideas to change the environment around you or the ecosystem, a foundation programme is a good start to a degree in the built environment. The foundation is designed to encompass all aspects of creative and critical thinking through design development, theory and skill-based modules, while the learner is given a sound understanding of the historical background of the built environment, its applications and other related aspects. The industry offers excellent career prospects and a fully-trained graduate will have no problem landing a lucrative job in his or her chosen field of study.
